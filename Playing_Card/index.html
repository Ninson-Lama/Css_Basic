This layout demonstrates multiple levels of Flexbox: The outer container (#playing-cards) centers and wraps the cards, and each inner card uses `flex-direction: column` and `justify-content: space-between` to position the header/footer elements, while the individual `left`, `middle`, and `right` divs use `align-self` to place themselves on the cross axis.
